Realms of Candarion Charter

V. Legislature

a. Each noble has votes in the legislature in accordance with (Rank section vote benefits)
b. Any noble may put forth a motion for voting in the legislature
c. Voting thresholds
  • i. A motion to amend the Charter requires a supermajority vote
  • ii. Any other motion requires a majority vote
d. Motions are open for voting for 14 days.
